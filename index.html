Time series analysis

Time series is a sequence of data points or observations collected or recorded at successive points in time, typically with equal intervals between them, and is used to analyze and understand patterns, trends, and behaviors over time.

Synthetic data

The generation of synthetic data is crucial as it enables the creation of diverse and representative datasets, facilitating robust model training and testing, especially in scenarios where real data is limited or sensitive.

Metalearning

Meta-learning, or "learning to learn", involves employing knowledge extracted from tasks, algorithms, or performance evaluations of models on past datasets to enhance performance, making it faster and more efficient for new datasets.
